Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Data Collection and Storage

BlueBuzzah retains the following information exclusively on your device:

  • Therapy session records
  • Vibration patterns and configurations
  • BlueBuzzah glove device settings
  • Application preferences

No data leaves your device. No cloud storage. No external servers.

Data Transmission

The application communicates solely via Bluetooth with paired BlueBuzzah therapeutic gloves. Internet connectivity is neither required nor used for any app functionality.

Personal Information

BlueBuzzah does not collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage metrics
  • Health records beyond locally stored session information

No account creation required. No sign-up process.

Third-Party Access

No third parties receive information from BlueBuzzah. The application avoids integration with:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage providers
  • External APIs

Data Control

Users have complete control over their data:

  • View all stored information within the app
  • Delete individual sessions or all data
  • Maintain exported data locally
  • All associated data is removed upon uninstallation

Children's Privacy

BlueBuzzah does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The application's local-only architecture ensures no personal data is transmitted regardless of user age.

Security

Data protection relies on your device's native security mechanisms. We recommend enabling device-level protections such as passcodes or biometric authentication to safeguard your therapy data.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Policy updates will be posted on this page with revised effective dates. Continued use of BlueBuzzah applications signals acceptance of any changes.

Compliance

This policy addresses GDPR, CCPA, and applicable data protection regulations through our privacy-by-design architecture.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact us at: privacy@bluebuzzah.com
